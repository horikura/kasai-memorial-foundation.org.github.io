Kasai Memorial Foundation

Preserving historical records, connecting memories, and passing them on to future generations.

The Kasai Memorial Foundation was established on 21 July 2026 as a cultural and historical initiative based in Gifu City, Japan.

We seek to research, record and communicate the history, people, places and local memories associated with the Kasai clan. We distinguish verified facts, scholarly interpretations and oral traditions, and we revise our content when reliable new evidence becomes available.

MISSION

Preserve records

We organise documents, photographs, oral accounts and local historical information while respecting ownership, copyright and privacy.

EDUCATION

Make history accessible

We present historical topics in clear language through websites, videos, articles, exhibitions and learning activities.

COMMUNITY

Build connections

We welcome information, cooperation and participation from residents, researchers, cultural organisations and future generations.
